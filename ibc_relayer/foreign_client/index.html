Module ibc_relayer::foreign_client

source ·
Expand description

Queries and methods for interfacing with foreign clients.

The term “foreign client” refers to IBC light clients that are running on-chain, i.e. they are foreign to the relayer. In contrast, the term “local client” refers to light clients running locally as part of the relayer.
